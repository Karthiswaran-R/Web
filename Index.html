Who We Are

VLSI Design Hub is a platform dedicated to helping students, engineers, and researchers excel in VLSI (Very Large Scale Integration) design. Our mission is to provide valuable resources, tools, and guidance for designing digital circuits, setting up Linux-based VLSI environments, and developing projects in Verilog, SystemVerilog, and RTL design.
